## Deferred material

- **Trinomial × binomial multiplication** (m82507) — the rewrite mentions the technique (distribute every term) but does not develop a separate worked example. Reason: once distribution is established for binomial × binomial, the larger case is mechanical.
- **Quotient-to-Power Rule** as a standalone subsection (m82509) — rewrite includes it in the integer-exponents table in §4 but does not give a separate worked example. Reason: the rule is structurally identical to product-to-power, and one example covers both.
- **Multiple long-division worked examples** (m82513) — rewrite gives one ($x^2 + 5x + 6$ ÷ $x + 2$). Reason: long division's algorithm is short; one example with explicit step-by-step is sufficient for the chapter's spine. More practice belongs in exercises.
- **Detailed conversion drills for scientific notation** (m82514) — rewrite gives a few examples and a synthesis use (Earth-Sun light travel). Drill-style conversions are deferred to exercises.

## Source-level notes

- **No contradictions** between source files.
- **Scientific notation conventions.** Source (and rewrite) uses the standard $1 \le |a| < 10$ with integer exponent. This is the conventional definition; the alternative *engineering notation* (where the exponent is a multiple of 3) is not covered here.
- **Pizza synthesis (§1) and baseball trajectory (§5).** Original constructions in the rewrite. Pizza area ratios verified: $49\pi / 25\pi = 1.96$, and $22/12 = 1.833 \approx 1.83 = 83\%$ price increase. Baseball coefficients ($-16$ for half-gravity in feet, $80$ for initial velocity, $4$ for initial height) match standard physics-textbook setups.
- **Earth-Sun calculation (§5).** Distance $1.496 \times 10^{11}$ m and $c = 3.00 \times 10^8$ m/s are textbook standard values. Computed light time $\approx 499$ s = 8 min 19 s matches the well-known figure of ~8 minutes 20 seconds.
