## Source-level notes

- **No contradictions** between source files were observed.
- **Pedagogical compression.** Source treats percent, percent change, simple interest, discount, and markup as five separate skills with worked examples for each (~6,800 words). Rewrite compresses to one template with variants noted (~700 words). Same content; tighter exposition.
- **Mixture problem.** The pharmacy saline example is original to this rewrite; OpenStax uses a coffee-bean mixture (kept as Synthesis exercise 9) and an investment-mix example (kept implicit in the template description).
- **Round-trip synthesis.** §5 uses a quadratic-producing motion problem to motivate chapter 10. The setup is faithful to OpenStax's treatment of round-trip problems (m82477) but the specific framing — a music festival, two friends — is original.
- **Pythagorean historical note.** Babylonian-numerical-instances claim is well-attested in mathematical history (Plimpton 322 tablet, ~1800 BCE). Standard.
